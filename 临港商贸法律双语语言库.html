临港涉外商贸法律标准双语句式库

点击展开/收起
1. 合同管辖通用条款
中文：本合同受中华人民共和国法律管辖并据其解释，不适用境外法律。
English: This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the People's Republic of China, excluding foreign laws.
2. 临港保税合规条款
中文：本次保税交易全程遵守上海临港新片区海关保税监管管理办法及相关合规规定。
English: This bonded transaction shall fully comply with the bonded supervision measures and relevant compliance regulations of Lingang New Area Customs.
3. 争议解决条款
中文：因本合同引起的争议，双方应协商解决；协商不成的，提交中国国际经济贸易仲裁委员会仲裁。
English: Any dispute arising from this contract shall be resolved by both parties through negotiation; if no settlement is reached, it shall be submitted to CIETAC for arbitration.
4. 不可抗力声明条款
中文：遭受不可抗力一方应及时通知对方，并在合理期限内提供有效证明文件。
English: The party suffering from force majeure shall notify the other party promptly and provide valid certification within a reasonable period.
5. 离岸贸易合规承诺条款
中文：本公司承诺所开展的离岸转手买卖业务背景真实、单证齐全、资金与货物流相匹配。
English: The company undertakes that the offshore entrepot trade conducted has genuine background, complete documents and matching capital and cargo flows.
6. 跨境电商责任条款
中文：卖方应保证跨境电商商品符合进口国质量标准及海关监管要求。
English: The seller shall ensure that the cross-border e-commerce commodities comply with the quality standards of the importing country and customs supervision requirements.
7. 保密义务条款
中文：双方应对合作过程中获悉的商业秘密及涉密信息承担永久保密义务。
English: Both parties shall bear permanent confidentiality obligations for trade secrets and confidential information obtained in the cooperation process.
8. 支付与结算条款
中文：买方应通过自由贸易账户完成跨境支付，结算货币为美元/人民币。
English: The buyer shall complete cross-border payment through a free trade account, and the settlement currency is USD/RMB.
